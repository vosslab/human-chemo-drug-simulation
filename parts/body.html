Chemotherapy body simulation

Track how a regimen rises, spreads, and clears over time.

This teaching model shows drug concentration as a function of time for a simplified human body. It emphasizes trends across bloodstream, liver, kidneys, and tumor exposure, with a stochastic response view for tumor shrinkage over a longer treatment window.

  • Two named regimen presets with a 30-day simulation window.
  • Synchronized concentration chart and stylized body-flow visual.
  • Manual dose timing plus patient survival feedback.
Educational use only

This page is an illustrative teaching tool. It does not predict a real patient's drug levels and must not be used for treatment or dosing decisions.
